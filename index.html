Frequently Asked Questions about AeroPaper

What is AeroPaper?

AeroPaper is a free, premium Pinterest-style wallpaper gallery created by wondermayank. It hosts over 1,500 high-quality Anime wallpapers, Aesthetic backgrounds, and animated GIF wallpapers in JPG, PNG, WEBP, and GIF formats. You can search, filter, preview, download, and edit wallpapers directly in the browser with no registration required.

Are the wallpapers on AeroPaper free to download?

Yes. All wallpapers in the AeroPaper gallery are completely free to download for personal use. Simply open any wallpaper in the lightbox viewer, click the Download button, and it will save directly to your device automatically.

What wallpaper formats does AeroPaper support?

AeroPaper supports five image and animation formats: JPG, JPEG, PNG, WebP, and GIF (animated). You can filter the gallery to show only wallpapers in a specific format using the Format pills at the top of the page.

Can I use AeroPaper wallpapers on my phone (9:16 portrait format)?

Yes. AeroPaper includes a built-in Canvas Wallpaper Editor. Open any wallpaper, click "Edit Wallpaper", choose the 9:16 (Phone) aspect ratio, and enable the "Blurred Fit" option. This creates a phone-optimized wallpaper with a beautifully blurred backdrop version of the desktop wallpaper perfectly fitted for your mobile home screen.

How many wallpapers are available on AeroPaper?

AeroPaper currently indexes 1,598 wallpapers across three categories: Anime Wallpapers, Anime Backgrounds, and Classic Wallpapers. New wallpapers are added periodically via the GitHub repository at github.com/wondermayank/wallpaper.

Does AeroPaper support animated GIF wallpapers?

Yes. AeroPaper hosts animated GIF wallpapers clearly tagged with a GIF badge in the masonry grid. Filter to show only GIFs using the Format filter. GIFs are served via GitHub Pages CDN for fast, edge-cached loading.

How can I start a wallpaper slideshow?

Click the Slideshow button in the stats bar at the top of the page or inside any wallpaper's detail panel. A fullscreen slideshow begins with automatic 4-second transitions and smooth fade effects. Use Arrow Left and Right keys to manually navigate, Space to pause, and Esc to exit.

Can I create custom boards and collections of wallpapers?

Yes. Inside any wallpaper's detail panel, you can click the + button to create a custom named board such as "Cyberpunk" or "Minimalist", then add that wallpaper to it. Boards are saved in your browser's localStorage and persist between sessions.

Who created AeroPaper?

AeroPaper was created by wondermayank, an independent developer based in India. You can visit their portfolio at https://wondermayank.in or their GitHub profile at https://github.com/wondermayank.
